What We Believe

Everyone in the community may not belong to the church, but the church belongs to the community. We applaud the businesses that hire from their individual resource to those needs surrounding their service population.

New Day - New Season

Today the Mt. Joy Baptist has risen to the call of God for this Millenial Generation. Building partners, with a Network of Ministry and Corporate services that will liberate families and translate into the challenge of the Great Commission.

Our church mission is to ignite a passion to follow Jesus.

The Pastor believes that the church (The Ecclesia) must rise from the traditions and sameness of past generations while having learned and gained from its spirited wisdom. We must press forward with a paradigm of inclusive change. A change that will launch us forward to grow and benefit from the differing expressions that were once disallowed.

This is Just the Beginning

I lift up the people of the Mt. Joy Baptist Church who serve as progenitors of a new and different direction. They remain prayer warriors. There us a shaking of monumental proportions at the door. In the Name of Jesus.
